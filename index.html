For Organizations

Using The Signal Room With Your Leadership Team

The conversations on this show are designed to surface the tradeoffs that rarely make it into a boardroom slide. Assign a specific episode to your leadership team each month as part of your AI governance learning agenda. Use the show notes and newsletter issues as a foundation for structured discussion around readiness, risk, and decision rights.

Several health system leaders have used episodes covering AI verification, data governance, and clinical trust as a starting point for internal alignment conversations before major AI investments. The goal is not consumption. The goal is a better-informed leadership team that can ask harder questions and make more durable decisions.

Who This Show Is For

This Is for You If...

You are a C-level or senior leader in a healthcare system, health tech company, or payer who is responsible for AI readiness, governance, and operational risk. You are not looking for vendor pitches or enthusiasm about AI's potential. You are looking for the harder questions, the real tradeoffs, and the perspective of peers who are accountable for the same kinds of decisions you are.

The leaders who get the most from this show tend to be the ones who already know enough to be frustrated by surface-level content. This show is built for that audience.

"Technology does not replace judgment. It amplifies it, or exposes the absence of it."

Chris Hutchins, Founder and CEO, Hutchins Data Strategy Consultants
