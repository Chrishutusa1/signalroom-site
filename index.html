About The Signal Room

The Signal Room is a podcast dedicated to exploring healthcare AI leadership, ethics, and strategy. I bring together leaders, innovators, and practitioners to discuss the real challenges of AI readiness, governance, and implementation in healthcare.

New episodes drop every Wednesday. I cover the topics that matter: healthcare AI ethics, LLM strategy, governance frameworks, clinical integration, and the human elements that make AI transformation successful.

About Chris Hutchins

Chris Hutchins is the Founder and CEO of Hutchins Data Strategy Consultants (HDSC). For nearly three decades, he has worked inside complex healthcare systems, advising organizations, technology companies, and investors on AI readiness, governance frameworks, and responsible innovation.

His work centers on a straightforward principle: technology does not replace judgment. It amplifies it, or exposes the absence of it. Chris educates senior leaders on proper AI implementation so they can protect trust, reduce risk, and lead through technological change with steadiness and perspective.
