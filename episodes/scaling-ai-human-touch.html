Show Notes

How to scale AI in healthcare without losing the human touch. Dr. Barry Chaiken examines how AI is redefining the patient-physician relationship and the leadership decisions that shape responsible adoption in clinical environments. This inaugural episode of The Signal Room sets the foundation for the conversations that follow.

AI in healthcare holds enormous promise, but scaling it responsibly requires understanding the dynamics of the patient-physician relationship. Technology that disrupts trust between clinicians and patients creates more problems than it solves, regardless of its technical performance. The leaders who get AI adoption right are the ones who start with the human relationship and work backward to the technology.

Topics covered: scaling AI in healthcare, the patient-physician relationship, responsible AI adoption, clinical trust and technology, healthcare leadership, and the human side of digital transformation in care delivery.
